✅ DO’s
Think before you post – Make sure your content is respectful and appropriate.
Maintain a professional profile – Use a decent bio and profile picture.
Verify information before sharing – Avoid spreading fake news or misinformation.
Respect others’ privacy – Ask permission before posting someone else’s photo.
Use strong privacy settings – Control who can see your posts and personal details.
Engage positively – Comment and interact in a polite and constructive way.
❌ DON’Ts
Don’t share personal/sensitive information – Avoid posting phone number, address, etc.
Don’t post offensive or abusive content – It can damage your reputation.
Don’t overshare your daily life – It can risk your safety and privacy.
Don’t engage in online arguments or trolling – Stay calm and avoid negativity.
Don’t click suspicious links – They may lead to scams or hacking.
Don’t ignore digital footprint – What you post stays online permanently.
